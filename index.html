Our Beliefs

We believe that solar energy can be the primary source of energy, a large and untapped resource in the Middle East region. Our vision is to lead the region into making solar energy as the primary source of energy in a way that is economically feasible, environmentally friendly, reliable, and practical. This will be done through technological innovation, public awareness and institutional support that is being driven by a realization renewable energy is imperative to sustain the environment and healthy economics. We strongly believe that it is not only the technology that results in success, rather the people who apply the technology to result in optimal results for our customers. To that effect, greenessence will develop, market, implement, and service solar technologies leveraging the region's solar concentrations to improve the living conditions of the people and societies. greenessence will be a private sector center of excellence for developing and improving the feasibility and reliability of solar energy technologies.
